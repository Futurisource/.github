# Futurisource  — Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Futurisource  is a community built on respect, collaboration, and shared growth. We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, experience level, nationality, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, religion, or background.

We are developers helping developers. That starts with how we treat each other.

---

## Our Standards

### ✅ We expect:
- Welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect for differing skill levels and opinions
- Constructive, kind feedback on code and ideas
- Patience with beginners — we were all beginners once
- Crediting others for their contributions and ideas
- Assuming good intent before reacting

### ❌ We do not tolerate:
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory jokes or language
- Dismissing or belittling contributions ("this is too simple")
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Spam, self-promotion unrelated to the community
- Claiming others' work as your own

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Futurisource  spaces, including:
- All GitHub repositories (issues, PRs, discussions, comments)
- Discord server (all channels, DMs within the server context)
- Social media when representing Futurisource 
- Public events or Tool Jams hosted by the community

---

## Reporting

If you witness or experience a violation, please report it:

- **Discord DM** a Moderator or Core Member
- **Email:** [conduct@futurisource.dev](mailto:conduct@futurisource.dev)

All reports are confidential. We will review every report and respond within 48 hours.

---

## Enforcement

Consequences for violations, depending on severity:

| Level | Violation | Consequence |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Minor, first-time | Private warning |
| 2 | Repeated or moderate | Temporary mute/ban from Discord |
| 3 | Severe or malicious | Permanent ban from all community spaces |

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject any contribution that violates this Code of Conduct.
